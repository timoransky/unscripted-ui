## How it works

- `appearance-none` clears the native look; `checked:bg-primary` paints the box.
- The check glyph is a sibling SVG faded and scaled in with `peer-checked:opacity-100` +
  `peer-checked:scale-100` — `pointer-events-none` keeps it from stealing clicks.
- Wrapping everything in a `<label>` makes the text clickable with zero attributes.
  Keyboard, forms, indeterminate state — all still native.
