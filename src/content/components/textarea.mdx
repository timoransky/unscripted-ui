## How it works

- `field-sizing-content` (`field-sizing: content`) makes the textarea size itself to its value.
  No hidden mirror element, no `scrollHeight` measuring, no resize listener.
- `rows="3"` sets the starting height, `max-h-48` caps the growth — past that it scrolls.
- Browsers without `field-sizing` simply keep the fixed `rows` height and scroll. Nothing to
  polyfill.
