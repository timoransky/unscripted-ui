## How it works

- Three stacked sections with `p-6` and trimmed inner edges — no dedicated CardHeader/CardContent
  components, just padding.
- The settings rows are the [Switch](/components/switch) component verbatim, with
  `justify-between` to push the toggle to the edge — composition is copy-paste here.
- Use semantic headings (`h3` or whatever fits your page outline) — the card doesn't care.
